About Our Currency Converter

Our Currency Converter is a simple and easy-to-use web application that allows you to convert between different currencies quickly and accurately. We offer real-time exchange rates for over 180 currencies, including major currencies like US dollar, Euro, British pound, and AUD dollar, as well as exotic currencies like the Bitcoin, the Kazakhstani tenge, and the Mongolian tugrik.

Our Currency Converter is built using the latest web technologies, including HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. We use the Currencyapi.com API to retrieve real-time exchange rates for all currencies.

Our Currency Converter is completely free to use, and there are no limits on the number of conversions you can perform. We also don't collect any personal information about our users, so you can use our service with confidence and peace of mind.
